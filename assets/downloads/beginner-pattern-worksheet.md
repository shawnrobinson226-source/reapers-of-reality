# Reapers of Reality — Beginner Pattern Worksheet

## Purpose

Use this worksheet to identify one repeating pattern, interrupt it clearly, and choose one action that holds.

## 1. Pattern Inventory

What keeps repeating?





Where does it usually show up?





What do you usually do when it appears?





## 2. Blade Question

What is the cleanest truth I am avoiding?





## 3. The Gap

What is the difference between what I say I want and what I keep doing?





## 4. Action That Holds

What is one action I can take today that proves the pattern is being interrupted?





The action must be small, specific, visible, and done within 24 hours.

## 5. Weekly Review

What changed this week?





What repeated?





What needs to be corrected next?





## Closing Standard

Do not perform change. Prove it in the next action.
